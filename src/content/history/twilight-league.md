## History of the ALCC Twilight League

Since the 1970s, American Legion Country Club has played host to a men's
Twilight League that begins on Thursdays in May and concludes in August or
September. The league has been run predominantly by its members for the
past 25 years. In 2009, the league was renamed the **Van Montague Memorial
Twilight League**, in honor of a league member and longtime supporter who
passed away.

The league consists of 6–12 teams with 8-man rosters. Schedules are built
to alternate nines each week from front to back. Handicaps are calculated
weekly to ensure competitive, fair play. Each week, two teams of four
golfers go head-to-head in A-B-C-D format, with skill prizes and a weekly
skins game.

In 2018, the league went on hiatus and was replaced with a weekly
Hollywood-style league.
