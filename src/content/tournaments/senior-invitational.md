## About This Event

The Senior Invitational was first introduced at American Legion Country
Club in 2019, quickly establishing itself as a highlight of the late
summer schedule. Created to showcase the lifelong enjoyment of the game, it
brings together golfers age 50 and older for a day of spirited competition
and camaraderie.

From its inception, the Invitational has featured an 18-hole better ball
of partners, stroke play format, with teams flighted by their 9-hole
performance. Optional side games like skins, pins, and team pools have
become a player favorite.

Today, the Senior Invitational stands not only as a competition, but as a
celebration of friendship, tradition, and the timeless spirit of golf at
ALCC.
