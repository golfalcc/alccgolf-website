## About This Event

Launched in 2015, the Fall Classic quickly became one of the marquee events
at American Legion Country Club. Known as a late-season showdown, it
offered golfers one final chance to compete for a major title before the
close of the year. For nearly a decade, the format was an 18-hole two-man
better ball event, with flighting determined by scores on nine randomly
chosen holes.

In 2024, the Fall Classic entered a new era — expanding into a two-day
event. Saturday remains a traditional better ball round, while Sunday now
features a club-favorite 18-hole Shootout, scramble style.

## How to Enter

Applications and GolfGenius sign-up links are posted each season on the
club's Facebook page and in the pro shop. Contact the pro shop at
(814) 542-4343 for the current entry fee and field size.
