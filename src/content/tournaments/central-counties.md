## About the Central Counties Golf Association

ALCC's CCGA Delegate is **Darold Boone** (cwbfan77@hotmail.com). The Central
Counties Golf Association is a long-running regional series bringing
together golfers from 14 member clubs across the region: American Legion
Country Club, Belles Springs, Clearfield-Curwensville, Down River,
Huntingdon Country Club, Iron Masters, Lewistown Country Club, Lost Creek,
Mountain View, Nittany Country Club, Park Hills, Philipsburg Country Club,
Sinking Valley, and Summit Country Club.

## Paul Bradley Central Counties Junior Scholarship

The CCGA offers the Paul Bradley Junior Scholarship to member-club juniors
in grades 9–12 who have played in at least two CCGA events during the
season. Applications are due June 1 of a student's senior year — contact
the pro shop or CCGA delegate for the current application.

_Edit `src/content/tournaments/central-counties.md` each season to update
the schedule and scholarship deadline._
